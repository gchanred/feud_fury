FEUD FURY - Instruction Manual


Introduction

Feud Fury is an engaging, real-time survey game where two teams compete to guess the most popular answers to survey questions. This game is controlled by a moderator while contestants follow along on a separate display. Enjoy the excitement at parties, family gatherings, or team-building events!

Getting Started

  1. Access the Game: Open your browser and go to the moderator view (e.g., https://feudfurygame.chandukesireddy.com/). For contestants, use https://feudfury.onrender.com/contestant in another window or share the URL.
  2. No Login Required: Simply visit the URL to join the game.

Moderator Interface

  • Round Setup: Enter team names and choose questions manually or use the “Choose 4 Random Questions” button to let the system pick four questions at random.
  • Starting the Game: Click “Start / Next Question” to begin or advance through the game.
  • Show Question: Click “Show Question to Contestants” to display the question on the contestant screen.
  • Reveal Answers: Click “Reveal” next to an answer to display it with audio cues.
  • Strikes & Steals: Mark a wrong answer by clicking “Strike.” After three strikes, a steal attempt can be made using the provided buttons.
  • Face-Off / Play-Pass: When the first answer is revealed, choose the winning team and whether they will “Play” or “Pass” to determine control.
  • Score Updates: Scores update in real time, but you can also manually adjust them using the “Update Scores” button.

Contestant Interface

  • The contestant screen shows a large “FEUD FURY” title and team scores in a fixed header.
  • Once the moderator displays a question, it appears in the center along with the answers (revealed as they are announced).
  • Strikes are shown as red “X” characters below the question and answers; a giant red “X” briefly appears when new strikes occur.
  • The winner is announced when the game is complete.

General Tips

  • Helper Icons: Hover over ℹ️ icons next to buttons for tooltips explaining their function.
  • Audio Controls: The moderator can mute or unmute background music using the dedicated button.
  • Real-Time Updates: Changes in the game state are pushed in real time—no manual refresh is needed.

Troubleshooting

If you experience any issues, ensure your browser supports WebSockets and that your network isn’t blocking Socket.IO connections.

Enjoy playing Feud Fury and have fun competing with your friends and family!
